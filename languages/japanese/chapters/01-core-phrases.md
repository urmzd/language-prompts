# Core Communication Phrases

This chapter provides essential phrases for everyday interactions in Japan, organized by communication scenario. Each section includes **pattern phrases** (templates with slots you can fill in) and **fixed phrases** (ready-to-use expressions).

---

## Pattern Phrase Templates

These reusable patterns let you express many ideas by swapping words in the slots marked with `[brackets]`.

### Requesting Something

| Pattern | Japanese | Romaji | Example |
|---------|----------|--------|---------|
| [thing] please | [もの]をください | [mono] o kudasai | 水をください (mizu o kudasai) = Water please |
| Please [action] | [動詞-て形]ください | [verb-te]kudasai | 待ってください (matte kudasai) = Please wait |
| Could you please [action]? | [動詞-て形]いただけますか | [verb-te] itadakemasu ka | 教えていただけますか (oshiete itadakemasu ka) = Could you please teach me? |
| I would like [thing] | [もの]がほしいです | [mono] ga hoshii desu | これがほしいです (kore ga hoshii desu) = I would like this |
| I want to [action] | [動詞-stem]たいです | [verb-stem]tai desu | 食べたいです (tabetai desu) = I want to eat |

### Asking Permission

| Pattern | Japanese | Romaji | Example |
|---------|----------|--------|---------|
| May I [action]? | [動詞-て形]もいいですか | [verb-te] mo ii desu ka | 写真を撮ってもいいですか (shashin o totte mo ii desu ka) = May I take a photo? |
| Is it okay if I [action]? | [動詞-て形]もよろしいですか | [verb-te] mo yoroshii desu ka | 入ってもよろしいですか (haitte mo yoroshii desu ka) = Is it okay if I enter? |
| Can I [action]? | [動詞]ことができますか | [verb] koto ga dekimasu ka | 払うことができますか (harau koto ga dekimasu ka) = Can I pay? |

### Asking Questions

| Pattern | Japanese | Romaji | Example |
|---------|----------|--------|---------|
| Where is [place]? | [場所]はどこですか | [basho] wa doko desu ka | トイレはどこですか (toire wa doko desu ka) = Where is the toilet? |
| How do I get to [place]? | [場所]へはどう行きますか | [basho] e wa dō ikimasu ka | 駅へはどう行きますか (eki e wa dō ikimasu ka) = How do I get to the station? |
| Do you have [thing]? | [もの]はありますか | [mono] wa arimasu ka | 英語メニューはありますか (eigo menyū wa arimasu ka) = Do you have an English menu? |
| What is [thing]? | [もの]は何ですか | [mono] wa nan desu ka | これは何ですか (kore wa nan desu ka) = What is this? |
| How much is [thing]? | [もの]はいくらですか | [mono] wa ikura desu ka | これはいくらですか (kore wa ikura desu ka) = How much is this? |
| What time does [place] [action]? | [場所]は何時に[動詞]ますか | [basho] wa nanji ni [verb]masu ka | お店は何時に閉まりますか (omise wa nanji ni shimarimasu ka) = What time does the shop close? |

### Stating Preferences and Needs

| Pattern | Japanese | Romaji | Example |
|---------|----------|--------|---------|
| I don't eat [food] | [食べ物]を食べません | [tabemono] o tabemasen | 肉を食べません (niku o tabemasen) = I don't eat meat |
| I'm allergic to [thing] | [もの]のアレルギーがあります | [mono] no arerugī ga arimasu | 卵のアレルギーがあります (tamago no arerugī ga arimasu) = I'm allergic to eggs |
| Without [thing] please | [もの]抜きでお願いします | [mono]-nuki de onegaishimasu | にんにく抜きでお願いします (ninniku-nuki de onegaishimasu) = Without garlic please |
| I'm looking for [thing] | [もの]を探しています | [mono] o sagashite imasu | ホテルを探しています (hoteru o sagashite imasu) = I'm looking for a hotel |

## Greetings and Basic Courtesies

### Daily Greetings

| English | Japanese | Romaji | When to Use |
|---------|----------|--------|-------------|
| Good morning | おはようございます | ohayō gozaimasu | Until ~10 AM |
| Hello / Good afternoon | こんにちは | konnichiwa | Daytime (~10 AM - 6 PM) |
| Good evening | こんばんは | konbanwa | Evening (after ~6 PM) |
| Goodbye | さようなら | sayōnara | Formal goodbye |
| See you later | また後で | mata ato de | Casual, same day |
| See you | じゃあね | jā ne | Very casual |
| Goodnight | おやすみなさい | oyasuminasai | Before sleeping |

### Thank You Expressions

| English | Japanese | Romaji | Formality |
|---------|----------|--------|-----------|
| Thanks | ありがとう | arigatō | Casual |
| Thank you | ありがとうございます | arigatō gozaimasu | Polite (most situations) |
| Thank you (past) | ありがとうございました | arigatō gozaimashita | After receiving service |
| Thank you very much | どうもありがとうございます | dōmo arigatō gozaimasu | Very polite |

### Apologies and Excusing Yourself

| English | Japanese | Romaji | When to Use |
|---------|----------|--------|-------------|
| Excuse me / Sorry | すみません | sumimasen | Getting attention, mild apology |
| I'm sorry | ごめんなさい | gomen nasai | Apology (casual-polite) |
| I'm very sorry | 申し訳ございません | mōshiwake gozaimasen | Formal apology |
| Excuse me (passing) | 失礼します | shitsurei shimasu | Passing someone, entering room |

### Responding to Thanks

| English | Japanese | Romaji | Formality |
|---------|----------|--------|-----------|
| You're welcome | どういたしまして | dō itashimashite | Standard |
| Not at all | いいえ | iie | Casual |
| No problem | 大丈夫です | daijōbu desu | Common response |

## Meeting People and Small Talk

### Introductions

| English | Japanese | Romaji | Notes |
|---------|----------|--------|-------|
| Nice to meet you | はじめまして | hajimemashite | First meeting |
| I'm [name] | [名前]です | [namae] desu | State your name |

| Please treat me well | よろしくお願いします | yoroshiku onegaishimasu | Said after introducing yourself |
| Where are you from? | どちらからですか | dochira kara desu ka | Polite way to ask |
| I'm from [country] | [国]から来ました | [kuni] kara kimashita | Stating your origin |

### Small Talk Questions

## Asking for Help

### Getting Attention

| English | Japanese | Romaji | Context |
|---------|----------|--------|---------|
| Excuse me | すみません | sumimasen | Standard, all situations |
| Excuse me (shop staff) | すみません | sumimasen | Call for attention |
| Hello? (service counter) | ごめんください | gomen kudasai | Traditional, at entrances |

### Requesting Assistance

| English | Japanese | Romaji | Formality |
|---------|----------|--------|-----------|
| Can you help me? | 助けていただけますか | tasukete itadakemasu ka | Very polite |
| Please help me | 助けてください | tasukete kudasai | Polite, urgent |
| Help! | 助けて！ | tasukete! | Emergency |
| I need help | 助けが必要です | tasuke ga hitsuyō desu | Stating need |
| I'm in trouble | 困っています | komatte imasu | Explaining situation |
| I'm lost | 道に迷いました | michi ni mayoimashita | When lost |

### Communication Difficulties

| English | Japanese | Romaji | Notes |
|---------|----------|--------|-------|
| I don't understand | わかりません | wakarimasen | Very useful |
| I don't speak Japanese | 日本語が話せません | nihongo ga hanasemasen | Explaining |
| Do you speak English? | 英語を話せますか | eigo o hanasemasu ka | Asking for English |
| Please speak slowly | ゆっくり話してください | yukkuri hanashite kudasai | Request slower speech |
| Please say that again | もう一度言ってください | mō ichido itte kudasai | Ask for repetition |
| Please write it down | 書いてください | kaite kudasai | Very helpful |
| Can you show me? | 見せていただけますか | misete itadakemasu ka | Request demonstration |
